From ancient temples to modern skylines


Interesting sights of Azerbaijan:

  • Location: Baku
  • Historical

One of Baku's most iconic landmarks, this
mysterious 12th-century tower stands at the edge
of the Old City. Its exact original purpose remains
unknown, making it one of Azerbaijan's greatest
historical puzzles.

  • Location: Baku
  • Modern

Three soaring skyscrapers that light up
Baku's skyline every night in a stunning LED
display resemblingflickering flames. They
have become the symbol of modern Azerbaijan.

  • Location: Baku
  • Relegious

Built around a naturally burning gas vent, this ancient
temple was once a sacred pilgrimage site for Hindu, Sikh,
and Zoroastrian worshippers. A truly one-of-a-kind sight you
won't find anywhere else.

  • Location: Gobustan
  • Nature, Historical

Home to over 6,000 rock carvings dating back
40,000 years, as well as bubbling mud volcanoes.
A UNESCO World Heritage Site that feels like
stepping into prehistoric times.

  • Location: Sheki
  • Historical

A breathtaking 18th-century royal residence
famous for its intricate stained glass windows,
detailed frescoes, and stunning geometric
patterns— all built without a single nail.

  • Location: Baku
  • Modern

A long, breezy promenade stretching along the
Caspian Sea shore. Perfect for a casual stroll, it offers
beautiful views of the water and the city's skyline side by side.
